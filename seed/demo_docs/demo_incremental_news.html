AlphaProteo: generative design of de novo protein binders

DeepMind research note — demo document for the incremental ingest pipeline.

AlphaProteo is a generative model that designs novel protein binders directly from a target structure, achieving experimentally validated binding affinities without iterative screening. This note summarizes the approach for evaluating the GE Search Portal's incremental (catalog-driven) ingestion path.

Key points

  • Designs high-affinity binders to a target protein from its 3D structure.
  • Reduces wet-lab iterations versus traditional directed-evolution screening.
  • Relevant to therapeutic antibody and binder discovery workflows.
